Fuel Price Watch Dog

This is an Open Source project dedicated to providing real-time fuel price transparency across Australia.

GitHub Repository:
https://github.com/DynastyKids/NodeJS-FuelWatchdog

Credits & Licenses

  • Node.js: Server-side runtime (OpenJS Foundation).
  • Leaflet: Interactive map engine (BSD-2-Clause).
  • MongoDB: Database engine (SSPL/Apache).
  • External Services: Navigation by Google Maps & Waze.

Data Attribution

This project uses open data provided by Australian State Governments:

  • NSW / ACT / TAS: FuelCheck data provided by API.NSW with supported by Supported by: Department of Customer Service NSW.
  • QLD: Fuel price data provided by State of Queensland (Department of Energy and Climate).
  • SA: Fuel price data provided by South Australia Government via fuel pricing scheme.
  • WA: Data sourced from WA FuelWatch, Department of Mines, Industry Regulation and Safety.
  • VIC: Fuel price data provided by Service Victoria, fuel price data may delay up to 24 hrs as required.
  • NT: Sourced via respective state-mandated real-time reporting schemes.

Note: The data is provided "as is" without warranty. Users should verify prices at the station.
